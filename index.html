How to get THINGS done?

Dursun Software Consulting is a sole proprietorship formed to provide consultation and independent contractorship services to international clients and comply with the laws in Turkey.

The legal presence of the company allows us to make independent contractor and software consultancy contracts with international companies and to do this in accordance with tax laws. Documents for the services we perform and the payments we receive in return are used by the accountant to manage the taxation processes within the company. The monthly full-time working wages specified in the working conditions section reflect the gross amount, and its 20% share is deducted as withholding tax, depending on Turkey's taxation laws.

If you are a company that does not have a legal entity in Turkey and makes its services available abroad, you do not have any VAT liability in Turkey for the services we provide to you. However, it would be useful to check the data of the legal authorities required for VAT obligations in your home country. Many states do not include any withholding tax or VAT in agreements with independent contractors abroad. But for exceptional cases, please apply to the necessary authorities in your own country.

In independent contractor agreements, we state that we do not have any authority for tax liabilities of the receiving party, that we do not provide consultancy services on such matters, and that we do not accept any responsibility for the consistency and accuracy of the taxes of the service recipient in their own country. Please contact us via the contact form at the bottom of the page for more detailed information about the services we perform and how the company processes the fees, documents and information provided from your side.
